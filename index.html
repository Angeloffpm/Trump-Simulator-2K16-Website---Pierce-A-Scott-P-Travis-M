About Trump Simulator 2K16

Trump simulator is the newest and hottest game on the market. In this super exciting action adventure thriller, you play as Trump himself, and have to collect the final brick to build his ultimate wall. Though, the people of Mexico have planted fake bricks along your construction site, and you need to make sure you only use the most solid of bricks. Will you collect the final brick and lock down the southern border?

.

Game Rules

The idea of the game is simple. As Donald Trump, there are bricks all scattered around the Mexican border, but Donald only uses the strongest bricks to build his wall. It's a race against the clock to find the real brick to finish up your wall and keep the Mexicans out of your great country, you have 60 seconds!
